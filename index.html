How to Play & Secrets 💡
everything you need to know about your starry adventure
🎮 How to Play

• Move your mouse to guide your cat through the stars

• Catch falling candies to earn points and build a streak

• Collect 💌 Love Notes (50 total) drifting through the sky

• Watch for rare 🌟 Special Notes from Grandpa (25 total)

• Build streaks to see beautiful combo effects!

🌟 Secrets & Hidden Features

• Collect ALL 75 notes to unlock a special secret cat!

• Look for the 💌 Message in a Bottle - it appears once per session

• Complete the finale to unlock a beautiful surprise ending

• Memory candies (special glowing ones) give you extra rewards

• Golden streak pips appear at 5, 10, and 15 consecutive catches

💙 Tips for Success

• Focus on maintaining your streak - it builds combo multipliers

• Check the journal to track your progress on all notes

• Notes appear more frequently as you play longer

• The secret cat has special glowing effects!
